Work with Us

OUR PROCESS

01

CONSULTATION

During our free initial consultation, we’ll discuss all your specifics needs, ideas, project scope, budget and timeline. We will also walk you through our design and build process and answer any questions or concerns you may have.

DESIGN + PROPOSAL

02

At this stage, our skilled team of builders and designers will work closely with you to create the custom build/renovation that you envisioned! Once the design is complete, we will provide a construction proposal and estimate. 

03

PROJECT PLANNING + BUDGET

Once the budget is confirmed and contract is signed we begin securing all the required permits and organize our designers, builders, trades, and project manager for your project. At this stage, the construction schedule will be set based off your timeline.

04

CONSTRUCTION

During construction we’ll keep you up to date by maintaining constant and clear communication - keeping you informed on the progress at each step of the way.

05

POST CONSTRUCTION

Once construction is complete, your project manager will inspect the project ensuring everything is completed in alignment with the design. At this point you and your project manager will do a walkthrough to identify any deficiencies or corrections and if any are identified they will be corrected to your approval.
